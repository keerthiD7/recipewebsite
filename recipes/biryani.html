Biryani

Description

Biryani is a popular Indian rice dish made with long-grain basmati rice, spices, and either chicken, mutton, or vegetables. The rice and masala are cooked separately and then layered together, which gives the biryani its unique aroma and flavor. It is enjoyed across India with different regional variations.

This biryani recipe is perfect for family meals or special occasions. The mix of spices, tender meat, and flavored rice makes it rich, delicious, and satisfying. Serve it hot with raita or salad for the best taste.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Curd / Yogurt
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook it until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and fry sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell goes away.
  4. Mix in chicken pieces, tomatoes, curd, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until the chicken becomes tender.
  5. In a deep pot, layer half of the rice, then add the chicken mixture, and cover with the remaining rice.
  6. Sprinkle mint and coriander leaves on top, cover with a tight lid, and cook on low heat for 15â€“20 minutes.
  7. Once done, gently mix the rice and chicken, and serve hot with raita or salad.
