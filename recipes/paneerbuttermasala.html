Paneerbuttermasala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy and rich North Indian curry made with soft paneer cubes, tomatoes, butter, and mild spices. It is one of the most popular dishes served in restaurants and pairs well with roti, naan, or rice.

This recipe gives you a smooth, flavorful gravy with a hint of sweetness and a buttery taste. It is perfect for lunch, dinner, or any special occasion. Even beginners can make this dish easily at home.

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cashews
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala
  • Fresh cream
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan, saut√© onions until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cashews, cook until soft, then blend into a smooth paste.
  3. Heat butter again, add ginger garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add the blended paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt; cook until oil separates.
  5. Add paneer cubes and garam masala, mix gently.
  6. Pour in fresh cream and crushed kasuri methi, simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
